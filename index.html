4. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

WePlugU does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our App, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

5. Data Security:

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Data Privacy Policy or as required by applicable laws. When your data is no longer needed, we will securely dispose of it.
